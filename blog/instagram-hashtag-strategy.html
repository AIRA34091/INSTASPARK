Why Hashtags Still Matter

Despite rumors of their demise, hashtags remain one of the most powerful organic discovery tools on Instagram. Posts with optimized hashtags receive up to 12.6% more engagement.

Strategic hashtag use boosts reach and engagement

Types of Hashtags to Use

A winning strategy uses multiple hashtag types:

  • Industry/Niche: #fitness, #travelblogger
  • Content-Specific: #homeworkout, #veganrecipe
  • Community: #fitnesscommunity
  • Location: #nyceats, #londonblogger
  • Branded: Your unique campaign tags

The 3-Tier Hashtag Strategy

The most effective strategies use a mix of hashtag sizes:

Tier 1: High-Volume (1M+ posts)

Use 3-5 popular hashtags. Massive reach but high competition.

Tier 2: Medium-Volume (100K-1M)

Use 10-15 moderately popular hashtags. Good balance of reach and competition.

Tier 3: Low-Volume (under 100K)

Use 10-15 niche hashtags. Easy to rank, highly engaged audiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many hashtags should I use?

Research suggests 20-25 hashtags is optimal for most accounts.

Should hashtags go in caption or comments?

Both work, but captions ensure immediate indexing. Try both and test results.
