Free Design Tools

You do not need expensive software to create stunning visuals. These free design tools rival paid alternatives:

Canva

The go-to tool for social media graphics, presentations, and marketing materials. The free version is incredibly powerful.

GIMP

A free, open-source alternative to Photoshop for advanced image editing.

Free design tools can create professional-quality graphics

Free Video Tools

Video editing does not have to be expensive:

CapCut

A powerful free video editor with professional features including auto-captions and effects.

OBS Studio

Free software for screen recording and live streaming.

Free Analytics Tools

Track your performance without paid subscriptions:

Native Platform Analytics

Instagram Insights, YouTube Analytics, and TikTok Analytics provide valuable data for free.

Google Analytics

Essential for tracking website traffic from social media.
